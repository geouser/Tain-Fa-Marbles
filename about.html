About Us

Established in the year 2000 has been dealing in various types of Marbles and Granite stones. The company situated in Sharjah, U.A.E. has four stock yards with a total area of 1,40,000 sq. ft. Al Asal Marbles has it's own granite processing units known as Asian Rocks Pvt. Ltd. and Gallant Granites Pvt. Ltd. in India. The company also have strong tie-ups with other major suppliers in India, China, Pakistan, Spain, Portugal, Greece, Turkey, Brazil, Oman and Vietnam.

Due to our assured material supply and satisfactory relationship with the customers, Al Asal Marbles has made phenomenal growth ever since it's inception and now enjoys the reputation of being one of the leading suppliers of building stones in domestic U.A.E. market and in other countries all over the middle east.

Our strength lies in availability of stocks of all kinds of materials as per the requirements of the customers with guaranteed quality at most competent prices.

We are capable of meeting any large requirements of our customers within shelled time. With our professional staff and marketing strategy which emphasizes on customer satisfaction, Al Asal Marbles is a home for all our customers. We are smoothly adapting to continues changes, transformations and demands of the market to meet our customers expectations.

BEST QUALITY, COMPETENT PRICE, FAIR & SINCERE DEALINGS WITH COMPLETE COMMITMENT is our MOTTO. We welcome all your valuable suggestions to further improve our products and service.
